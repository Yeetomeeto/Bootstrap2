super park

i had visited on 4/4/2022

SuperPark is a new all-in-one indoor activity/playground park with 3 themed areas that offer over 20 fun, healthy and energizing activities, including trampolines, soccer and basketball courts, slides, obstancle cources and many more. Adults can also come as there is no height or age limit. This indoor park is also suitable for 3 year old children as there is a seperate section for little children. It is in Suntec city, so getting there by MRT or bus is not a problem.

Established in January 1984, POPULAR Book Company (M) Sdn Bhd has become a household name in Malaysia and has now grown to more than 97 stores nationwide. It is recognised and listed by the Malaysia Book of Records as the largest bookstore chain in Malaysia, with over 800,000 square feet of retail space. POPULAR Holdings is a Singapore-based company that publishes, distributes, and retails books for both the educational and general fiction markets in Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong and other parts of the world.
